ACCOUNT TRANSFER INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Using your preferred web browser, open two (2) new tabs or windows.
  2. In the first browser tab/window, navigate to: https://www.hotmail.com and click the "Sign in" button near the top-right.
    Sign in to outlook using the username and password provided in your access key.

    *NOTE: If you see a Microsoft notice that says the account has been LOCKED, then you will need to click the "NEXT" button and follow the on screen instructions to unlock the account by providing a phone number that can receive text messages. After completing this process, you will gain access to the email account.

  3. Switch to the second browser tab/window and navigate to: https://www.epicgames.com and click the "SIGN IN" link at the top-right.
    Sign in to epic games using the username and password provided in your access key.
    Once signed in to the epic games site, hover your mouse over the account name at the top-right of the screen.
    In the pop-up menu that appears, click the "ACCOUNT" link.

  4. From the menu on the left side, select the "PASSWORD & SECURITY" option.
    Enter the password used to log in to epic games in the "CURRENT PASSWORD" field.
    Enter a new password in the "NEW PASSWORD" field. This will be your new password you will sign in with.
    Enter it again in the "RETYPE NEW PASSWORD" field.
    Click the "SAVE CHANGES" button.
  5. From the menu on the left side, select the "GENERAL" option.
    Scroll down to the section "CONTACT AND ADDRESS INFORMATION"
    Click the "EMAIL ADDRESS" field and enter the email address you would like to transfer the account to.
    Click the "CONFIRM" button to proceed and a security code will be sent to outlook.
  6. Switch to the first browser tab/window and you should see an email from epic games with a security code in it.
    Open the email by clicking on it and copy the security code.
  7. Switch to the second browser tab/window and enter the security code in the "SECURITY CODE" field.
    Click the "CONTINUE" button to proceed.

The account will now be transferred to your new email address.
You can change the rest of the account information (username, country, etc.) to your own details.
